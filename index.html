Innovation is Incremental in Creation

But Revolutionary in Perception

Innovation is extremely attractive. Every company wants to make path breaking innovations and change the way we do things. But what every company, and every person who actually innovates, does is contribute and add to a repository of shared knowledge. Everyone adds a few pieces to the puzzle, including the last one who completes the puzzle. However, the public perception and popular intuition entirely rests on the success, and not the journey. The journey can be grey, but success is binary, its either success or failure. Hence, when a company fits the last piece into the puzzle, it is regarded as revolutionary, whereas, it has just placed a few more pieces into a puzzle being solved for decades by hundreds of companies and people.

Joining Travelxp brought you to the door of innovation. However, taking the keys and opening the door is an onus up to you.
